EGUSI SOUP RECIPE

Egusi is one of the mainy nigeria deliceies. it is made from freishly grown naturalingridents.

Ingridents

  • egusi(melon)seed
  • meat
  • stoke fish
  • dry fish
  • palm oil
  • onion
  • peper
  • crayfish
  • meat stock
  • seasoning
  • bitterleaf
  • salt

steps to follow

  1. prepare your Ingridents
  2. steam the fish and meat
  3. blend the peper
  4. prepare egusiplate
  5. heat palmoil
  6. fry the egusi
  7. add peper
  8. add stock
  9. add meat and fish
  10. add water to adjust tickness to your taste
  11. add seasoning
  12. add vegatibles
  13. allow to cook
  14. serve
